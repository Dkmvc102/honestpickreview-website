## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- Self-leveling function saves time versus manual bubble levels
- Projects both horizontal and vertical lines simultaneously
- Magnetic mounting bracket attaches to metal studs and fixtures
- Compact size fits easily in a tool bag

### Cons

- Self-leveling locks up if tilted beyond a few degrees, requiring manual adjustment
