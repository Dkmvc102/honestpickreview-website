FlexCore Adjustable Dumbbell Set is best suited for: **Home gyms with limited space replacing a full dumbbell rack**.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- Replaces a large rack of fixed-weight dumbbells in a small footprint
- Quick dial adjustment between sets saves time
- Durable plate mechanism rated for regular home use
- Comes with a storage tray for easy weight changes

### Cons

- Dropping them is not recommended and may damage the adjustment mechanism
