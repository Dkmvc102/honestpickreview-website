## Cons

1. **This is genuinely premium pricing.** Roof boxes and bike racks in the $750-$1,300+ range are a serious investment compared to budget cargo carriers — justified for frequent, serious use, less so for a once-a-year trip.
2. **Correct installation and weight compliance are essential for safety.** Roof-mounted cargo systems require following your specific vehicle's rack weight rating and the product's installation instructions precisely — this isn't optional given the safety stakes of loose or overloaded roof cargo.
3. **Mounted cargo boxes affect driving dynamics.** Expect some wind noise, handling changes, and reduced fuel economy while installed — factor in whether you'll remove it between uses.
