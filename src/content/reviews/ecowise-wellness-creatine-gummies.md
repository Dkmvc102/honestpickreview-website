## Overview

EcoWise Wellness sells creatine in gummy form — positioned as a mess-free alternative to mixing creatine powder into water or shakes.

## Product Lineup

### Creatine Monohydrate Gummies
Micronized creatine monohydrate, sugar-free, dosed at 4 gummies (5g creatine) per serving. **Price:** $45/month on subscription.

### Creatine HCl Gummies
A creatine hydrochloride version, marketed as an alternative form to monohydrate. **Price:** $48/month on subscription.

**[→ Compare Monohydrate vs. HCl gummy pricing](https://ecowisevitamins.com?sca_ref=11734467.odVyXLAOoWN4)**

### Longevity Foundation & Ashwagandha Gummies
Separate supplement blends sold as their own product lines, priced $23–$40/month.

## Who It's Best For

Gym-goers and athletes who already know they want creatine supplementation but dislike the texture or mixing hassle of powder.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- Creatine monohydrate has the most research behind it of any form; HCl is marketed on solubility/bloating claims that are less extensively studied independently.
- Gummy format means more sugar-alcohol/binder content per dose than plain powder, even in a "sugar-free" formulation.
- The advertised prices are subscription rates — confirm the actual one-time price and cancellation terms before subscribing.

## Our Verdict

EcoWise Wellness is a reasonable choice for creatine supplementation if convenience matters more than getting the absolute lowest cost per gram — plain powder remains cheaper per dose for those who don't mind mixing it.
