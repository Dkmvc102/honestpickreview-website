**Is this suitable for a weeklong trip?**

At 40L it is best suited for 1–3 night trips; longer trips generally call for 50L or more depending on how much gear you carry.

**Does it fit a hydration bladder?**

Yes, there is an internal sleeve and hose port for a hydration bladder up to 3 liters.
