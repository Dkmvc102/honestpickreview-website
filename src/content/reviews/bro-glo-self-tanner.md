## Who It's Best For

Men new to self-tanning who want a product formulated specifically for typical male skin and application habits, rather than a unisex or women's-marketed self-tanner.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- Patch-test before full application, especially on facial skin, since DHA reactions vary by individual.
- Application technique (exfoliating first, using the included mitt) affects streaking more than the specific product used.
- Marketing claims like "nobody will know it's fake" reflect brand positioning, not a guarantee — results vary by skin tone.

## Our Verdict

Bro Glo is a solid option for men specifically looking for a self-tanner formulated and marketed for their needs, with reasonable bundle pricing. As with any self-tanner, a patch test and proper application technique matter more than brand choice for avoiding streaking or an unnatural tone.
