If most clothing with "meaningful" graphics feels like a licensed slogan slapped on a stock tee, Octopied Mind takes a genuinely different approach — original hand-drawn art from a queer, Indigenous, and female-owned label built specifically around self-expression.

## What Is Octopied Mind?

Octopied Mind Inc. is an art-driven, gender-neutral clothing label that transforms original hand-drawn art into wearable pieces, sized XS through 6X. The brand is queer, Indigenous, and female-owned, with a stated mission centered on helping people free themselves from negative beliefs through self-expression.
