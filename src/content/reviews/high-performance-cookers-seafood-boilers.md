## Cons

1. **This is genuinely expensive equipment.** A full bundle runs $846.98, and commercial units reach into the thousands — this needs to be a real, regular-use purchase to justify the cost.
2. **The headline performance figures are manufacturer claims.** They're specific and testable, which is a good sign, but your actual results will vary with burner setup, altitude, water volume, and starting conditions — treat the numbers as strong directional claims rather than guaranteed exact outcomes.
3. **Casual or infrequent boilers may not need this tier of equipment.** If you host one boil a year, a basic turkey fryer setup may deliver adequate results for a fraction of the cost.
