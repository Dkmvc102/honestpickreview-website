## Who It's Best For

Families who want an easier way to cover nutritional gaps than swallowing pills, particularly parents looking for kid-friendly vitamin formats.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- Supplements aren't a substitute for a balanced diet — NutriQ's gummies are marketed as filling nutritional gaps, not replacing whole-food nutrition.
- Check ingredient lists for allergens and any conflicts with medications, especially for children's formulas.
- Talk to a pediatrician or doctor before starting any new supplement for a child.
- Stock availability changes — some items were listed as out of stock at the time of writing.

## Our Verdict

NutriQ is a reasonably well-positioned gummy vitamin brand for families wanting an easier supplement format, backed by clear dietary claims (vegan, gluten-free, sugar-free). As with any supplement, treat manufacturer health claims as marketing statements and consult a healthcare provider before starting a new regimen, especially for children.
