## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- Nano-mist is finer and gentler than boiling-water steam methods
- Adjustable steam nozzle angle for comfortable positioning
- Large water tank supports a full 15–20 minute session
- Quiet operation compared to some competing steamers

### Cons
- Requires distilled water for best performance and to avoid mineral buildup
- Steam alone does not replace proper extraction technique or professional facials for problem skin
- Tank needs regular cleaning to prevent mineral scale

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I add essential oils to the water tank?**

Most manufacturers advise against adding oils or fragrances directly to ultrasonic tanks, as they can damage the misting disc; check your specific model's instructions.

**How often should I use a facial steamer?**

Once or twice a week is a commonly recommended frequency to avoid over-drying or irritating skin.
