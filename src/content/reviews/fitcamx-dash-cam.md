## What Is FITCAMX?

FITCAMX makes integrated, vehicle-specific dash cameras designed to fit particular car models with a factory-installed look, using hidden wiring routed through the rearview mirror area instead of a visible cable run. The catalog covers 40+ brands, from mainstream models like Toyota and Ford to more niche fits like Porsche and RAM trucks.

## How It Works & What's Included

The camera unit mounts behind the rearview mirror, with wiring routed along the headliner and A-pillar trim rather than left exposed — the brand markets this as not requiring A-pillar removal or fuse box access, and an independent tester who tried it confirmed the install lived up to that claim. Footage is accessed through a companion smartphone app rather than a built-in screen, and most kits are available in front-only or front+rear camera configurations.

## Cons

1. **Trustpilot shows a rating around 2.6/5**, with a significant share of one-star reviews. Reading through them, the dominant complaints are about slow refunds and support response times, and frustration with the companion app — not widespread reports of the camera itself failing. That's a real signal worth taking seriously, but it points more at customer service capacity than a defective product, which is a meaningfully different risk profile.
2. **No GPS or speed overlay** on recorded footage — if you want embedded location/speed data for insurance or dispute purposes, this isn't built in.
3. **24/7 parking mode needs optional hardwiring.** The standard plug-and-play kit alone won't give you always-on recording while parked.
4. **Vehicle-specific fit is a double-edged sword** — the OEM look only works because it's built for your exact model, so it doesn't transfer if you sell the car and buy something FITCAMX doesn't support.

If a clean, OEM-style install is your top priority and you're comfortable relying on the companion app rather than a screen, it's a reasonable pick; if responsive customer service is non-negotiable for you, weigh the Trustpilot pattern carefully before ordering.
