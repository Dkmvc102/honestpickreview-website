Royal Clips sells professional-grade hair clippers, trimmers, and shavers for barbers and home grooming enthusiasts, with the Fade Kit 2.0 as its flagship three-piece bundle.

## What's in the Kit

The Fade Kit 2.0 combines the **RC-C2 Professional Clipper** (7,000 RPM brushless motor), **RC-T2 Precision Trimmer** (7,200 RPM, zero-gappable blade), and **RC-FS2 Foil Shaver** (dual foil system) — plus interchangeable limit combs, clipper oil, cleaning brushes, and charging bases.

**[→ Compare Fade Kit bundle options and pricing](https://royalclips.com)**

## Who It's Best For

People who cut their own or family members' hair regularly and want tools closer to professional barbershop equipment, rather than a basic drugstore clipper set.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- The kit's price reflects professional-grade motors and included accessories — infrequent users may not need this tier of equipment.
- Technique matters as much as tools for achieving clean fades — consider tutorial content alongside the purchase if you're new to fading.
- Check whether individual tools (just the clipper, for example) are sold separately if you don't need the full three-piece kit.

## Our Verdict

Royal Clips' Fade Kit 2.0 is a reasonable investment for regular home barbers who want cordless, higher-RPM tools closer to salon-grade equipment. Occasional users may find a simpler, cheaper clipper set sufficient for basic haircuts.
