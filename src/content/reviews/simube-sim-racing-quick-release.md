## What Is Simube?

Simube makes sim racing hardware, most notably its **QR2 quick-release system** for swapping steering wheel rims on direct-drive wheelbases, plus USB button box components (Powerlink) and dashboard display hardware (SDD). The brand positions itself as a more affordable alternative to premium OEM sim racing accessories while maintaining compatibility with major wheelbase ecosystems like Simucube.

## Cons

1. **A very small Trustpilot sample shows some real friction.** With only 7 reviews, at least one describes being denied a return and a disagreement over correct product usage — this is a genuine data point, but the sample is too small to draw firm conclusions from on its own, especially set against the much larger positive samples elsewhere.
2. **Shipping from China may take longer** than ordering from a domestic sim racing retailer, worth factoring into your timeline if you need parts quickly.
3. **Correct bolt pattern and installation matter.** The site offers both 50mm and 70mm compatibility with adapters — double-check your exact wheelbase's bolt pattern before ordering to avoid a compatibility mismatch.
