## Cons

1. **Torsion spring replacement carries real injury risk regardless of part quality.** These springs store significant tension energy, and mishandling them during installation can cause serious injury — this is true no matter how good the part itself is, so factor your own experience level into the decision, not just the product reviews.
2. **Compatibility errors are a real risk with any parts retailer.** Double-check your door and opener's exact brand and model before ordering, since a mismatched part means a return and a delay.
