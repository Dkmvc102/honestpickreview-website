## Who It's Best For

Homeowners comfortable with hands-on repairs who want to avoid a service call for straightforward garage door issues — best suited to extension spring or hardware replacement rather than high-tension torsion spring work unless you're experienced.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- **Torsion spring replacement carries real injury risk** — the springs store significant tension energy. If you're not experienced, consider hiring a professional for this specific repair even if you DIY other garage door maintenance.
- Double-check part compatibility against your door and opener's exact brand and model before ordering.
- Basic hardware (rollers, hinges, weather seals) is generally lower-risk and more approachable for a first DIY garage door project.
