## Overview

HBD Jewellery specializes in personalized pieces — necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and charms that can be customized with names, initials, or dates, handmade in the UK. The brand also runs sub-brands (Gigi & Maple, Noa & Nellie) at a slightly more premium tier.

**[→ Shop HBD's personalized jewelry](https://hbdjewellery.com)**

## Product Lineup

Rather than named product lines, HBD organizes its catalog by piece type: necklaces (including initial, name, and date pendants), bracelets (personalized charm and chain styles), earrings, and charms as add-ons for existing chains or bracelets.

**[→ See HBD's current necklace and bracelet pricing](https://hbdjewellery.com)**

## Who It's Best For

Gift buyers shopping for milestones (graduations, motherhood, weddings, anniversaries) who want a personalized touch, plus parents looking for children's jewelry.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- Vermeil (gold-plated sterling silver) is not the same as solid gold — factor that into comparisons across finishes.
- Confirm the return policy on personalized/engraved pieces specifically, since it may differ from non-personalized items.
- Check children's sizing against the brand's size guide rather than assuming adult sizing scaled down.
- The tiered multi-item discount is worth it mainly if you're already planning to buy more than one piece.

## Our Verdict

HBD is a solid pick specifically for personalized jewelry gifts, with a wide price range covering both budget and higher-end purchases. Compare vermeil vs. solid gold options carefully if long-term wear is a priority over upfront price.
