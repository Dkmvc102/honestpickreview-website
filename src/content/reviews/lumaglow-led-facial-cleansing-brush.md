A silicone facial cleansing brush with sonic vibration and optional LED light modes, intended as a gentle exfoliation step layered on top of your regular cleanser.

## Who It's Best For

LumaGlow LED Facial Cleansing Brush is best suited for: **Adding a deeper cleansing step to an existing skincare routine**.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- Silicone bristles are gentler on skin than nylon-bristle brushes
- Fully waterproof for use in the shower
- Multiple intensity levels accommodate sensitive skin
- USB rechargeable with a reasonably long battery life

### Cons
- LED light modes have limited independent clinical backing and should be treated as a bonus feature, not a primary treatment
- Not a replacement for actual cleanser, still needs to be paired with one
- Bristles should be replaced or cleaned regularly to avoid bacteria buildup

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use this every day?**

Most dermatology guidance suggests using facial cleansing brushes a few times a week rather than daily to avoid over-exfoliating, though this can vary by skin type.

**Do the LED modes actually treat skin conditions?**

LED light therapy at consumer-device intensities is generally considered a mild supplementary feature; it should not replace dermatologist-recommended treatments for specific skin concerns.
