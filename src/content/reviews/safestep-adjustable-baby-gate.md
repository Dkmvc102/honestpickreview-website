## Who It's Best For

SafeStep Adjustable Baby Gate is best suited for: **Blocking stairways and doorways for toddlers and pets**.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- One-handed opening is convenient while carrying a child
- Pressure-mount installation requires no wall drilling
- Extension kits available for extra-wide openings
- Auto-close and lock feature adds a safety layer

### Cons
- Pressure mounting is not recommended at the top of stairs; hardware mounting is safer there
- Can loosen over time and needs periodic re-tightening
- Step-over threshold may be a minor tripping consideration for adults

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can this be used at the top of stairs?**

Pressure-mounted gates are generally not recommended at the top of stairs; a hardware-mounted gate is the safer option there.

**Is it easy for a toddler to open?**

The one-handed mechanism is designed to be difficult for young children to operate while remaining quick for adults.
