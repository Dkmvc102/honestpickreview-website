## Cons

1. **This is a multi-thousand-dollar business investment**, not an impulse buy — you're committing $2,399 at minimum before earning a single dollar back, which is a real financial risk if local demand doesn't materialize.
2. **The kit is the start, not the finish** — you'll need to keep buying chain, jump rings, and findings as your starting inventory sells through, an ongoing cost beyond the initial purchase.
3. **Equipment quality doesn't guarantee business success.** Sunstone can hand you reliable welds and training, but finding customers, pricing your services, and marketing locally are still entirely on you.
