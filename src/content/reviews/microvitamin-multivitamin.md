## What Is MicroVitamin?

MicroVitamin was developed by Dr. Brad Stanfield, a New Zealand-based MD known online for evidence-based, anti-hype supplement commentary. The brand's entire positioning is built around transparency: publishing exact ingredient forms and doses instead of hiding amounts behind proprietary blend labeling, and backing that up with independent third-party testing rather than just brand claims.

## Key Ingredients & What Makes the Formulation Different

MicroVitamin's core formula combines **25 nutrients**, each dosed — according to the brand — at levels matching the doses used in published human clinical trials, not arbitrary "enough to put on the label" amounts. Specific formulation choices include:

- **Methylated B vitamins** (more readily usable by the body than standard folic acid/cyanocobalamin forms for people with common MTHFR gene variants)
- **Glycinate-form minerals** and **chelated minerals**, generally better absorbed than cheaper oxide forms
- **Microencapsulated carotenoids** including lutein/zeaxanthin for eye health
- **200mg hyaluronic acid** and **TMG (trimethylglycine)** in the formula, ingredients not commonly included in standard multivitamins

Independent verification matters here: MicroVitamin scored **99.7 out of 100 (Grade A)** on Labdoor, a third-party lab that tests supplements for label accuracy and contamination. That's a genuinely strong, externally verified result — not a number MicroVitamin generated itself.
