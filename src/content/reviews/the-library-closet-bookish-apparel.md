## Cons

1. **This is intentionally niche apparel.** The humor and fandom references only land if you know the specific books or communities — a risky gift choice unless you're confident the recipient will get the reference.
2. **You're paying a premium over generic graphic tees** — $29.99 and up for a basic design is meaningfully more than a plain screen-printed tee from a big-box retailer.
3. **Licensed items aren't permanent.** Fandom collections depend on ongoing licensing agreements, so a specific design available today isn't guaranteed to still be in stock (or licensed) next year.
