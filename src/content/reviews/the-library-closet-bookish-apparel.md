## Who It's Best For

Readers active in book communities (BookTok, Bookstagram, fantasy/romance fandoms) who want apparel that signals their specific reading interests, rather than generic "I love books" merchandise.

- Licensed fandom pieces are the most niche — make sure you're actually familiar with the referenced series before buying as a gift for someone else.
- The custom bookshelf sweatshirt design is a nice personalization option if you want something beyond a stock graphic, but expect a longer turnaround for customized items.
- Compare sizing charts per product line rather than assuming your usual size carries across tees, Comfort Colors shirts, and sweatshirts.
