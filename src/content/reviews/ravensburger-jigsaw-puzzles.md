If you've ever finished 95% of a jigsaw puzzle only to have pieces that don't quite lock together properly, Ravensburger built its reputation specifically around solving that exact frustration with proprietary piece-cutting technology.

## What Is Ravensburger?

Ravensburger is a long-established German puzzle and game manufacturer, known specifically for Softclick technology — a proprietary piece-cutting process designed to create a tighter, more secure fit between puzzle pieces than typical budget puzzles offer. The company's broader portfolio includes BRIO wooden train sets and construction toys.

## Cons

1. **Missing pieces is a real, documented complaint pattern** — a genuinely serious issue for jigsaw puzzles specifically, since even one missing piece undermines the entire completion experience.
2. **Piece-fit quality isn't perfectly consistent across every product**, with some reviewers reporting looser-than-expected fit on specific puzzles despite the Softclick technology positioning.
3. **Customer service response for replacement piece requests has been flagged as inconsistent** by some reviewers — worth being persistent if you do need to file a claim.

## What Customers Are Saying

Ravensburger holds a **3.7/5 rating across 745 Trustpilot reviews** — a meaningful sample for this category, with feedback that's specific and genuinely useful for evaluating the brand rather than generic praise or complaints. The recurring positive theme centers on piece-fit quality, directly validating the Softclick technology's core promise. The recurring negative theme is missing pieces upon opening a new puzzle — a real, serious issue specific to this product category, since it can render an otherwise good puzzle unusable. Against other premium puzzle brands, Ravensburger's combination of a large, independently verified review sample and long-standing category-specific reputation gives it a well-documented position, even accounting for the real missing-piece complaint pattern that affects puzzle brands generally.
