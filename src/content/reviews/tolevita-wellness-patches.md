## Who It's Best For

People who dislike swallowing pills or mixing powders and want to try a simpler patch-based format for common wellness goals, understanding that transdermal delivery is less researched than oral supplements for many active ingredients.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- Patch-based transdermal delivery is well-established for some compounds (like nicotine patches) but far less studied for others (like ashwagandha or GABA) — treat specific efficacy claims with appropriate skepticism.
- Patch-testing a small area first is a reasonable precaution given any adhesive can cause skin irritation in sensitive individuals.
- These are not a substitute for medical treatment (e.g., smoking cessation aids with clinical backing) if you're addressing a serious health goal — talk to a doctor for guidance.

## Our Verdict

TOLEVITA's patches are a low-commitment way to try wellness support without pills, best approached as a convenience-focused option rather than a clinically proven treatment. Reasonable to try for mild, non-medical goals; not a substitute for evidence-based treatment for more serious concerns.
