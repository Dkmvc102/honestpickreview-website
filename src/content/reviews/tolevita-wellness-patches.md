## Key Ingredients & How It Works

- **Sleep Patches** use melatonin and lavender, two ingredients with reasonable standalone research behind them for sleep support — though the transdermal delivery format specifically is less studied than oral melatonin.
- **Calm Patches** combine GABA, ashwagandha, L-theanine, passion flower, and magnesium — a broad herbal blend aimed at stress reduction.
- **Anti-Smoke Patches** come in multiple strengths (7mg, 14mg, 21mg) using a natural tobacco extract, positioned as a lower-intensity alternative to nicotine replacement patches.

Transdermal absorption itself is a proven mechanism for some compounds (nicotine patches are the well-established example), but far less researched for herbal actives like ashwagandha or GABA — worth keeping in mind when evaluating the specific efficacy claims.

## Cons

1. **An independent review rated the overall experience 6.5/10**, concluding TOLEVITA "sells real products" but that results are "inconsistent and not guaranteed," with marketing claims that can read as more confident than the underlying science supports.
2. **Transdermal research gap.** Some ingredients in these patches (nicotine-adjacent compounds) have decades of absorption research behind the patch format; others (ashwagandha, GABA, passion flower) mostly have oral-supplement research, which doesn't automatically prove they work the same way through skin.
3. **Mixed individual results and support concerns.** The same independent review flagged some users experiencing no noticeable effect, plus friction with customer service and refunds — worth knowing before ordering multiple packs at once.
