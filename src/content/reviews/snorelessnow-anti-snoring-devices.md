## Who It's Best For

People with mild, positional, or congestion-related snoring who want to try targeted over-the-counter devices before considering a medical evaluation.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- "FDA-cleared" (510(k) clearance) means the device was cleared as substantially equivalent to an existing device — it's not the same as the FDA independently verifying the brand's specific effectiveness claims.
- Snoring has many possible causes (sleep apnea, allergies, anatomy, weight, alcohol use, sleep position); a mouth guard or pillow may not address snoring from an underlying medical condition.
- Loud, chronic snoring with daytime fatigue or breathing pauses is a signal to see a doctor rather than relying solely on an over-the-counter device.

## Our Verdict

SnoreLessNow's product-per-cause approach is a reasonable structure for tackling common, mild snoring triggers. It's not a substitute for medical evaluation if snoring is severe or linked to sleep apnea symptoms.

This article is not medical advice. Consult a doctor or sleep specialist for diagnosis and treatment of snoring or suspected sleep apnea.
