## What Is Bro Glo?

Bro Glo sells water-based self-tanning products using DHA (dihydroxyacetone) derived from sugar beets, marketed as producing a natural bronze tone rather than an artificial orange cast. The brand gained visibility after an appearance on ABC's "Shark Tank" and cites 250,000+ customers and a 4.6/5 rating from 1,200+ reviews on its site.

## Product Lineup

### Face Tanner
A lightweight, water-based formula designed specifically for facial application — thinner than the body formula to avoid clogging pores or feeling heavy on facial skin.

### Body Tanner
A full-body bronzing foam designed for quick, even application across larger skin areas.

### Applicator Mitt
A heart-shaped mitt included to help apply the tanner evenly and reduce the risk of streaking or blotchy patches — a common complaint with self-tanners applied by hand.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- **Development time and duration are typical for DHA-based tanners** — expect roughly 6–8 hours to develop and 5–7 days of color, fading faster or slower depending on skin exfoliation and moisturizing habits.
- **Patch-test before full application**, especially on facial skin, since reactions to DHA-based formulas vary by individual.
- **Application technique affects results more than the product itself** for most self-tanners — using the included mitt and exfoliating beforehand tends to reduce streaking regardless of brand.
- Marketing claims like "nobody will know it's fake" reflect the brand's positioning, not a guarantee — results vary by skin tone and application.
