## Things to Know Before Buying

- **"FDA-cleared" refers to a specific regulatory pathway (510(k) clearance for Class II devices)** — it means the device was cleared as substantially equivalent to an existing device, not that the FDA has evaluated or endorsed the brand's specific effectiveness claims. Worth understanding the distinction before treating it as a blanket safety/efficacy guarantee.
- **Snoring has many possible causes** (sleep apnea, allergies, anatomy, weight, alcohol use, sleep position) — a mouth guard or pillow may not address snoring caused by underlying conditions like obstructive sleep apnea, which needs proper medical diagnosis.
- **If snoring is loud, chronic, or accompanied by daytime fatigue, gasping, or pauses in breathing during sleep**, that's a signal to see a doctor or sleep specialist rather than relying solely on an over-the-counter device.
- The brand's 120-day return policy is worth confirming directly on their site, since terms can change.
