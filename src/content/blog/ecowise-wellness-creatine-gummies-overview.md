## What Is EcoWise Wellness?

The brand's core product is creatine delivered as pre-dosed gummies rather than powder, using what it describes as "micronized" creatine for better solubility. It also sells a small range of complementary supplement gummies.

## Product Lineup

### Creatine Monohydrate Gummies
Micronized creatine monohydrate, sugar-free, dosed at 4 gummies (5g creatine) per serving. **Price:** $45/month on subscription.

### Creatine HCl Gummies
A creatine hydrochloride version, marketed as an alternative form to monohydrate. **Price:** $48/month on subscription.

### Longevity Foundation
A separate supplement blend marketed around general longevity support. **Price:** $40/month on subscription.

### Ashwagandha Gummies
An adaptogen supplement sold as its own product line. **Price:** $23/month on subscription.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- **Creatine monohydrate is one of the most researched sports supplements**, generally associated with strength and muscle-performance benefits in exercise science literature — but individual results vary, and it works alongside training and diet, not as a replacement for either.
- **Gummy format means more sugar/binder content per dose** compared to plain powder, even in a "sugar-free" formulation — check the ingredient list if you're tracking specific dietary factors.
- **Subscription-first pricing** — the advertised prices are subscription rates; one-time purchase (if available) may cost more. Confirm the actual checkout price and cancellation terms before subscribing.
- **HCl vs. monohydrate creatine** — monohydrate has far more long-term research behind it; HCl is marketed on claims of better solubility/less bloating, which are less extensively studied in independent research.
