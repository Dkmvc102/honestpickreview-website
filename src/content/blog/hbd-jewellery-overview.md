HBD Jewellery specializes in personalized pieces — necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and charms that can be customized with names, initials, or dates, handmade in the UK.

**[→ Shop HBD's personalized jewelry](https://hbdjewellery.com)**

## What Is HBD Jewellery?

The brand focuses on personalization as its core differentiator: most pieces can be customized with a name, initials, or a meaningful date. It offers gold, silver, and vermeil finishes, plus dedicated sterling silver options sized for children. HBD also runs sub-brands (Gigi & Maple, Noa & Nellie) positioned at a slightly more premium tier.

## Product Lineup

Rather than named product lines, HBD organizes its catalog by piece type:

- **Necklaces** — including initial, name, and date pendants
- **Bracelets** — personalized charm and chain styles
- **Earrings** — including studs and personalized charm styles
- **Charms** — add-on pieces for existing chains or bracelets

**[→ See HBD's current necklace and bracelet pricing](https://hbdjewellery.com)**

## Pricing

Listed prices range from £11.99–£133.99, with most popular pieces falling between £30–£75. The brand runs a tiered discount: 30% off a second piece, 60% off a third, and a free fourth piece when buying multiples.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- **"Vermeil" means gold-plated sterling silver**, not solid gold — worth knowing the difference if you're comparing pieces across finishes, since vermeil plating can wear thinner over years of daily use compared to solid gold.
- **Personalized/engraved items are typically final sale** at most jewelry retailers — confirm HBD's specific return policy on customized pieces before ordering, since it may differ from their policy on non-personalized items.
- **Sizing for children's pieces** (sterling silver collection) should be checked against the brand's size guide rather than assumed from adult sizing.
- The tiered multi-item discount only pays off if you're already planning to buy more than one piece — factor that into whether it's actually a good deal for your specific order.
