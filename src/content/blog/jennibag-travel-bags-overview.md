JenniBag makes carry-on-sized travel bags built around a core problem: fitting more into an airline-approved bag without paying checked-luggage fees. This overview covers their lineup based on the product information published on their official site.

## What Is JenniBag?

JenniBag focuses on duffel-style carry-on bags, offered in "Classic" and "Roller Edition" configurations across a few generations (1.0, 2.0, and Signature). The pitch is straightforward: an airline carry-on-sized bag engineered to hold significantly more than a typical duffel, so travelers can avoid extra luggage fees.

## Product Lineup

### JenniBag Classic (1.0 / 2.0 / Signature)
The core duffel-style carry-on, using water-resistant "TPU-900D Oxford Fabric" with compression panels and internal organizational compartments. Listed at roughly 2.2–3 lbs and sized around 20–21" L × 9" W × 12–13" H — within standard airline carry-on limits.

### Roller Edition
The same core design with a retractable handle and wheels, extending to about 45 inches with the handle up. Includes a trolley sleeve so smaller bags can attach on top when paired with checked luggage.

### Accessories
Backpacks, tote bags, crossbody bags, cosmetic bags, tech pouches, luggage tags, and packing cubes/vacuum compression bags round out the lineup for travelers who want matching organization.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- **Carry-on sizing varies by airline.** JenniBag markets its bags as carry-on approved, but budget airlines in particular have stricter (and sometimes smaller) size limits — measure your specific airline's policy before relying on it fitting.
- **"Fits 10-15+ days of clothing" is a marketing claim** dependent on packing style and how tightly compression panels are used — treat it as a rough upper bound, not a guarantee for every traveler.
- **Roller Edition adds weight** compared to the Classic duffel — worth considering if you plan to carry it up stairs or on uneven terrain rather than roll it.
- Check current warranty terms (the brand lists a 60-day risk-free guarantee and lifetime defect replacement) directly on the site before purchase, as policies can change.
