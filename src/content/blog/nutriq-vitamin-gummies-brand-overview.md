## What Is NutriQ?

NutriQ positions itself as a gummy-vitamin brand developed with input from German pediatricians, targeting families who want an easier way to cover nutritional gaps than swallowing pills or capsules. According to the brand, their gummies are:

- 100% vegan
- Gluten-free
- Non-GMO
- Sugar-free
- Formulated without unnecessary additives

These are manufacturer claims rather than independently verified facts — worth keeping in mind, as with any supplement brand.

## Things to Know Before Buying

- **Supplements aren't a substitute for a balanced diet.** NutriQ's gummies are marketed as filling nutritional gaps, not replacing whole-food nutrition.
- **Check ingredient lists for allergens** and any conflicts with medications, especially for children's formulas.
- **Talk to a pediatrician or doctor before starting any new supplement**, particularly for kids' products like the Calcium & Vitamin D3 or Multivitamin & Omega-3 lines — dosing needs vary by age and individual health status.
- **Stock availability changes.** At the time of writing, some items (like Kids Multivitamin & Omega-3 and Kids Complete) were listed as out of stock on NutriQ's site.
- Pricing shown reflects listed sale prices on NutriQ's site at the time of writing and may change — always check the current price before purchasing.
